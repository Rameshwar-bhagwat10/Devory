## Why Backlinks Matter Most

**Google's ranking algorithm prioritizes:**
1. **Backlinks (40%)** - How many quality sites link to you
2. **Content (30%)** - Quality and relevance of your content
3. **Technical SEO (20%)** - Site speed, mobile-friendly, structure
4. **User Signals (10%)** - Click-through rate, time on site, bounce rate

**You have #2, #3, and #4 covered. You NEED #1 (backlinks)!**
